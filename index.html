About Ngomongo

Built to Connect People and Empower Businesses

Ngomongo is a communication platform that offers fast, secure messaging, voice, and video calls. But we go beyond simple chats — we empower users to explore business profiles and products directly within the app.

Whether you're chatting with friends or browsing a local store's latest deals, Ngomongo brings everything into one seamless experience.

Our vision is to become the all-in-one platform where communication meets commerce. We believe that meaningful connection is the foundation of trust—between people and between people and brands.

We aim to redefine how people interact with businesses: simple, real-time, and personal.

Ngomongo started as a small idea: "What if chatting apps did more than just chat?" From that idea, we began building a platform in 2024 that merges communication with business discovery.

After months of development, research, and testing, Ngomongo was launched to make conversations meaningful again—whether between friends or between customer and business.

FAQ

Any Questions? Look Here

Find answers to the most frequently asked questions about our services.

Why am I not receiving notifications on some of my devices?

Please check the following: Make sure notifications are enabled under Settings > Notifications. → Ensure the app is not restricted by battery saver or background restrictions. → Grant the app permission to run in the background. If everything is set correctly but notifications still don’t appear, try logging out and logging back in, or update to the latest version of the app.

Can I use one account on two devices at the same time?

Yes, most chat apps support using your account on multiple devices like a phone and a computer. However, using two phones may have limitations. Check the Linked Devices feature in settings and enable two-step verification for added security.
